Ingredients:

  • Onions
  • Cans of tomatoes
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Meat of choosing
  • Lots of mozzy cheese
  • A deep oven safe container
  • Various herbs

Steps:

  1. First dice the onions, carrots and celery
  2. Fry it up with the meat
  3. Make a cheese sauce
  4. Layer alternativly the meat sauce, the cheese sauce and the pasta
  5. Bake at 200° for 45-55 Minutes
  6. Eat when cool
